He ended, and his words replete with guileInto her heart too easie entrance won:Fixt on the Fruit she gaz’d, which to beholdMight tempt alone, and in her ears the soundYet rung of his perswasive words, impregn’dWith Reason, to her seeming, and with Truth;Meanwhile the hour of Noon drew on, and wak’dAn eager appetite,
