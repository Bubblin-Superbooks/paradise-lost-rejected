Thus they in Heav’n, above the starry Sphear,Thir happie hours in joy and hymning spent.Mean while upon the firm opacous GlobeOf this round World, whose first convex dividesThe luminous inferior Orbs, enclos’dFrom Chaos and th’ inroad of Darkness old,Satan alighted walks: a Globe farr offIt seem’d, now seems a boundless ContinentDark, waste, and wild, under the frown of NightStarless expos’d, and ever-threatning stormsOf Chaos blustring round, inclement skie;Save on that side which from the wall of Heav’nThough distant farr som small reflection gainesOf glimmering air less vext with tempest loud:Here walk’d the Fiend at large in spacious field.As when a Vultur on Imaus bred,Whose snowie ridge the roving Tartar bounds,Dislodging from a Region scarce of preyTo gorge the flesh of Lambs or yeanling KidsOn
