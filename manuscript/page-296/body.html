O when meet nowSuch pairs, in Love and mutual Honour joyn’d?With Goddess-like demeanour forth she went;Not unattended, for on her as QueenA pomp of winning Graces waited still,And from about her shot Darts of desireInto all Eyes to wish her still in sight.And Raphael now to _Adam_’s doubt propos’dBenevolent and facil thus repli’d.
