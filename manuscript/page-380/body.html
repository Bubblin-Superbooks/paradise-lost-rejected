Soon as th’ unwelcome newsFrom Earth arriv’d at Heaven Gate, displeas’dAll were who heard, dim sadness did not spareThat time Celestial visages, yet mixtWith pitie, violated not thir bliss.About the new-arriv’d, in multitudesTh’ ethereal People ran, to hear and knowHow all befell: they towards the Throne SupreamAccountable made haste to make appearWith righteous plea, thir utmost vigilance,And easily approv’d; when the most HighEternal Father from his secret Cloud,Amidst in Thunder utter’d thus his voice.
