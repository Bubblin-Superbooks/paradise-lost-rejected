Back to the Thicket slunkThe guiltie Serpent, and well might, for EveIntent now wholly on her taste, naught elseRegarded, such delight till then, as seemd,In Fruit she never tasted, whether trueOr fansied so, through expectation highOf knowledg, nor was God-head from her thought.Greedily she ingorg’d without restraint,And knew not eating Death: Satiate at length,And hight’nd as with Wine, jocond and boon,Thus to her self she pleasingly began.
