Thus farr to try thee, Adam, I was pleas’d,And finde thee knowing not of Beasts alone,Which thou hast rightly nam’d, but of thy self,Expressing well the spirit within thee free,My Image, not imparted to the Brute,Whose fellowship therefore unmeet for theeGood
