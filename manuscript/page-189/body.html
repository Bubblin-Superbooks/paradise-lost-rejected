So spake th’ Eternal Father, and fulfilldAll Justice: nor delaid the winged SaintAfter his charge receivd, but from amongThousand Celestial Ardors, where he stoodVaild with his gorgeous wings, up springing lightFlew through the midst of Heav’n; th’ angelic QuiresOn each hand parting, to his speed gave wayThrough all th’ Empyreal road; till at the GateOf Heav’n arriv’d, the gate self-opend wideOn golden
