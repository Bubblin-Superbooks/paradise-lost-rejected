On th’ other side up roseBelial, in act more graceful and humane;A fairer person lost not Heav’n; he seemdFor dignity compos’d and high exploit:But all was false and hollow; though his TongueDropt Manna, and could make the worse appearThe better reason, to perplex and dashMaturest Counsels: for his thoughts were low;To vice industrious, but to Nobler deedsTimorous and slothful: yet he pleas’d the eare,And with perswasive accent thus began.
