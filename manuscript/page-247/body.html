So scoffing in ambiguous words, he scarceHad ended; when to Right and Left the FrontDivided, and to either Flank retir’d.Which to our eyes discoverd new and strange,A triple-mounted row of Pillars laidOn Wheels (for like to Pillars most they seem’dOr hollow’d bodies made of Oak or FirrWith branches lopt, in Wood or Mountain fell’d)Brass, Iron, Stonie mould,
