To whom thus Adam fervently repli’d.O Woman, best are all things as the willOf God ordaind them, his creating handNothing imperfet or deficient leftOf all that he Created, much less Man,Or ought that might his happie State secure,Secure from outward force; within himselfThe danger lies, yet lies within his power:Against his will he can receave no harme.But
