Like Maia’s son he stood,And shook his Plumes, that Heav’nly fragrance filldThe circuit wide. Strait knew him all the bandsOf Angels under watch; and to his state,And to his message high in honour rise;For on som message high they guessd him bound.Thir glittering Tents he passd, and now is comeInto the blissful field, through Groves of Myrrhe,And flouring Odours, Cassia, Nard, and Balme;A Wilderness of sweets; for Nature hereWantond as in her prime, and plaid at willHer Virgin Fancies, pouring forth more sweet,Wilde above rule or art; enormous bliss.Him through the spicie Forrest onward
