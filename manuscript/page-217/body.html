So spake the fervent Angel, but his zealeNone seconded, as out of season judg’d,Or singular and rash, whereat rejoic’dTh’ Apostat, and more haughty thus repli’d.That we were formd then saist thou? & the workOf secondarie hands, by task transferdFrom Father to his Son? strange point and new!
