Thrice happie men,And sons of men, whom God hath thus advanc’t,Created in his Image, there to dwellAnd worship him, and in reward to ruleOver his Works, on Earth, in Sea, or Air,And multiply a Race of WorshippersHoly and just: thrice happie if they knowThir happiness, and persevere upright.
