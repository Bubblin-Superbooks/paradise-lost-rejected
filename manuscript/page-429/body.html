So spake our Father penitent, nor EveFelt less remorse: they forthwith to the placeRepairing where he judg’d them prostrate fellBefore him reverent, and both confess’dHumbly thir faults, and pardon beg’d, with tearsVVatering the ground, and with thir sighs the AirFrequenting, sent from hearts contrite, in signOf sorrow unfeign’d, and humiliation meek.
