To whom thus Raphael answer’d heav’nly meek.Nor are thy lips ungraceful, Sire of men,Nor tongue ineloquent; for God on theeAbundantly his gifts hath also pour’d,Inward and outward both, his image faire:Speaking or mute all comliness and graceAttends thee, and each word, each motion formes.Nor less think
