He added not, for Adam at the newesHeart-strook with chilling gripe of sorrow stood,That all his senses bound; Eve, who unseenYet all had heard, with audible lamentDiscover’d soon the place of her retire.

O unexpected stroke, worse then of Death! Must I thus leave thee Paradise? thus leaveThee Native Soile, these happie Walks and Shades,Fit haunt of Gods? where I had hope to spend,Quiet though sad, the respit of that dayThat must be mortal to us both.
