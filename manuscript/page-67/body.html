Thus saying roseThe Monarch, and prevented all reply,Prudent, least from his resolution rais’dOthers among the chief might offer now(Certain to be refus’d) what erst they feard;And so refus’d might in opinion standHis rivals, winning cheap the high reputeWhich he through hazard huge must earn. But theyDreaded not more th’ adventure then his voiceForbidding; and at once with him they rose;Thir rising all at once was as the soundOf Thunder heard remote.
