As yet this world was not, and Chaos wildeReignd where these Heav’ns now rowl, where Earth now restsUpon her Center pois’d, when on a day(For Time, though in Eternitie, appli’dTo motion, measures all things durableBy present, past, and future) on such dayAs Heav’ns great Year brings forth, th’ Empyreal HostOf Angels by Imperial summons call’d,Innumerable before th’ Almighties ThroneForthwith from all the ends of Heav’n appeerdUnder thir Hierarchs in orders brightTen thousand thousand Ensignes high advanc’d,Standards, and Gonfalons twixt Van and ReareStreame in the Aire, and for distinction serveOf Hierarchies, of Orders, and Degrees;Or in thir glittering Tissues bear imblaz’dHoly Memorials, acts of Zeale and LoveRecorded eminent.
