I miss thee here,Not pleas’d, thus entertaind with solitude,Where obvious dutie erewhile appear’d unsaught:Or come I less conspicuous, or what changeAbsents thee, or what chance detains? Come forth.He came, and with him Eve, more loth, though firstTo offend, discount’nanc’t both, and discompos’d;Love was not in thir looks, either to GodOr to each other, but apparent guilt,And shame, and perturbation, and despaire,Anger, and obstinacie, and hate, and guile.Whence Adam faultring long, thus answer’d brief.
