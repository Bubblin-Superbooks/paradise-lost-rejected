Whereto thus Adam fatherly displeas’d.O execrable Son so to aspireAbove his Brethren, to himself affirmingAuthoritie usurpt, from God not giv’n:He gave us onely over Beast, Fish, FowlDominion absolute; that right we holdBy his donation; but Man over menHe made not Lord; such title to himselfReserving, human left from human free.But this Usurper his encroachment proudStayes not on Man; to God his
