Silence, ye troubl’d waves, and thou Deep, peace,Said then th’ Omnific Word, your discord end:

Nor staid, but on the Wings of CherubimUplifted, in Paternal Glorie rodeFarr into Chaos, and the World unborn;For Chaos heard his voice: him all his TraineFollow’d in bright procession to beholdCreation, and the wonders of his might.Then staid the fervid Wheeles, and in his handHe took the golden Compasses, prepar’dIn Gods Eternal store, to circumscribeThis Universe, and all created things:One foot he center’d, and the other turn’dRound through the vast profunditie obscure,And said, thus farr extend, thus farr thy bounds,This be thy just Circumference, O World.Thus God the Heav’n created, thus the Earth,Matter unform’d and void: Darkness profoundCover’d th’ Abyss: but on the watrie calmeHis brooding wings the Spirit of God outspred,And vital
