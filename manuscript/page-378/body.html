Thus it shall befallHim who to worth in Women overtrustingLets her Will rule; restraint she will not brook,And left to her self, if evil thence ensue,Shee first his weak indulgence will accuse.

Thus they in mutual accusation spentThe fruitless hours, but neither self-condemningAnd of thir vain contest appeer’d no end.
