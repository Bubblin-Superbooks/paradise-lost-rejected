So spake our Sire, and by his count’nance seemdEntring on studious thoughts abstruse, which EvePerceaving where she sat retir’d in sight,With lowliness Majestic from her seat,And Grace that won who saw to wish her stay,Rose, and went forth among her Fruits and Flours,To visit how they prosper’d, bud and bloom,Her Nurserie; they at her coming
