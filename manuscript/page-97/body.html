Now had the Almighty Father from above,From the pure Empyrean where he sitsHigh Thron’d above all highth, bent down his eye,His own works and their works at once to view:About him all the Sanctities of HeavenStood thick as Starrs, and from his sight receiv’dBeatitude past utterance; on his rightThe radiant image of his Glory sat,His onely Son; On Earth
