So these the lateHeav’n-banisht Host, left desert utmost HellMany a dark League, reduc’t in careful WatchRound thir Metropolis, and now expectingEach hour their great adventurer from the searchOf Forrein Worlds: he through the midst unmarkt,In shew plebeian Angel militantOf lowest order, past; and from the doreOf that Plutonian Hall, invisibleAscended his high Throne, which under stateOf richest texture spred, at th’ upper endWas plac’t in regal lustre.
