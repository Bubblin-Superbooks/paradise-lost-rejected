Uzziel, half these draw off, and coast the SouthWith strictest watch; these other wheel the North,Our circuit meets full West. As flame they partHalf wheeling to the Shield, half to the Spear.From these, two strong and suttle Spirits he calldThat neer him stood, and gave them thus in charge.

Ithuriel and Zephon, with wingd speedSearch through this Garden, leav unsearcht no nook,But chiefly where those two fair Creatures Lodge,Now laid perhaps asleep secure of harme.This Eevning from the Sun’s decline arriv’dWho tells of som infernal Spirit seenHitherward bent (who could have thought?) escap’dThe barrs of Hell, on errand bad no doubt:Such
