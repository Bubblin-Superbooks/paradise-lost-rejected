LoinesSalvation shall be Preacht, but to the SonsOf Abrahams Faith wherever through the world;So in his seed all Nations shall be blest.Then to the Heav’n of Heav’ns he shall ascendWith victory, triumphing through the aireOver his foes and thine; there shall surpriseThe Serpent, Prince of aire, and drag in ChainesThrough all his realme, & there confounded leave;Then enter into glory, and resumeHis Seat at Gods right hand, exalted highAbove all names in Heav’n; and thence shall come,When this worlds dissolution shall be ripe,With glory and power to judge both quick & dead,To judge th’ unfaithful dead, but to rewardHis faithful, and receave them into bliss,Whether in Heav’n or Earth, for then the EarthShall all be Paradise, far happier placeThen this of Eden, and far happier daies.

So spake th’ Archangel Michael, then paus’d,As at the Worlds great period; and our SireReplete with joy
