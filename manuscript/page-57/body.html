Let us not then pursueBy force impossible, by leave obtain’dUnacceptable, though in Heav’n, our stateOf splendid vassalage, but rather seekOur own good from our selves, and from our ownLive to our selves, though in this vast recess,Free, and to none accountable, preferringHard liberty before the easie yokeOf servile Pomp.
