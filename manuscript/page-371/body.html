So said he, and forbore not glance or toyOf amorous intent, well understoodOf Eve, whose Eye darted contagious Fire.Her hand he seis’d, and to a shadie bank,Thick overhead with verdant roof imbowr’dHe led her nothing loath; Flours were the Couch,Pansies, and Violets, and Asphodel,And Hyacinth, Earths freshest softest lap.There they thir fill of Love and Loves disportTook largely, of thir mutual guilt the Seale,The solace of thir sin, till dewie sleepOppress’d them, wearied with thir amorous play.Soon as the force of that fallacious Fruit,That with exhilerating vapour
