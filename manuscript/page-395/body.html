With joyAnd tidings fraught, to Hell he now return’d,And at the brink of Chaos, neer the footOf this new wondrous Pontifice, unhop’tMet who to meet him came, his Ofspring dear.Great joy was at thir meeting, and at sightOf that stupendious Bridge his joy encreas’d.Long hee admiring stood, till Sin, his faireInchanting Daughter, thus the silence broke.
