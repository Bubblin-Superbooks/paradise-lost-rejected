He spake: and to confirm his words, out-flewMillions of flaming swords, drawn from the thighsOf mighty Cherubim; the sudden blazeFar round illumin’d hell: highly they rag’dAgainst the Highest, and fierce with grasped arm’sClash’d on their sounding shields the din of war,Hurling defiance toward the vault of Heav’n.
